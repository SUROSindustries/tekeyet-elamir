Our Story

Nestled in the heart of Siwa Oasis, Siwa Sands Caf√© brings together generations of traditional recipes with a modern touch. Our story began in 2010 when founder Amira returned to her ancestral home, determined to share Siwa's unique culinary heritage with the world.

We source our ingredients from local farmers and producers, ensuring each dish tells a story of the land and its people. From our organic dates to our hand-ground spices, every element reflects Siwa's rich culture and breathtaking landscapes.

More than just a restaurant, we're a gathering place where travelers and locals alike can connect over extraordinary food in an atmosphere that celebrates Siwa's timeless beauty.

Explore Siwa

While you're visiting us, don't miss these incredible Siwa attractions:

Shali Fortress

The ancient mud-brick fortress at the heart of Siwa, dating back to the 13th century. Climb to the top for panoramic views of the oasis.

Cleopatra's Spring

A natural spring where Cleopatra is said to have bathed. Enjoy a refreshing swim in the crystal-clear waters.

Great Sand Sea

Experience the breathtaking dunes of the Sahara with a sunset safari or overnight camping trip.
